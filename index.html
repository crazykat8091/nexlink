// Common Questions

FAQ

How do I pay?

I keep it simple: Cash, Zelle, or Venmo work best. I'll settle up with you once the job is finished and you're happy with how everything is working.

How much is this going to cost?

I don't like surprise bills either. I'll give you a clear quote based on what needs doing before I start any work. The initial $125 covers the house call and the time it takes to find the root of the problem.

What does the $125 cover?

That covers my travel and the "detective work" to find out exactly why your tech is acting up. Once I find the culprit, I'll give you a flat price for the fix. Even if you decide not to do the repair, you'll have an expert diagnosis and a clear plan of action.

I have a Mac—can you still help me?

Definitely. I’ve been working on Macs as long as I’ve been working on PCs. Whether it’s a MacBook that’s acting up or an iMac that needs a speed boost, I’m comfortable with the whole Apple ecosystem.

Can you show me how to use my new setup?

That's exactly why I'm here. I'm happy to sit down and walk you through everything so you feel confident using it on your own. There are no "silly" questions—if you want to know how something works, just ask!
